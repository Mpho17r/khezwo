Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2025

Welcome to KheZwo ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our QR code ordering platform.

1. Information We Collect

Vendors: When you sign up as a vendor, we collect your business name, owner name, email address, phone number, and password. You may also upload your logo and menu items with photos.

Customers: When you place an order, we collect your name, phone number, order details, and payment method. We do not store full credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our ordering platform
  • To process orders and notify vendors of new orders
  • To generate QR codes for your business
  • To improve our services and analyze usage
  • To send important updates about your account

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We also use Google AdSense to show ads. Google uses cookies to serve personalized ads based on your visits to our site and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense: Shows ads on customer menu pages. Google Privacy Policy
  • Render: Hosts our application. Render Privacy Policy
  • PostgreSQL (Aiven): Stores your data securely. Aiven Privacy Policy

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Passwords are hashed before storage. All data transmission uses encryption (HTTPS).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at hello@khezwo.com for any privacy requests.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify vendors of significant changes via email.
